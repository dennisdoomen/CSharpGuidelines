---
rule_id: 1608
rule_category: testability
title: Show what's important in a test, hide what's not
severity: 2
---
Every test has a specific intent. The data, setup and assertions that are directly relevant to that intent should be visible in the test body. Everything else should be hidden behind [Test Data Builders](https://wiki.c2.com/?TestDataBuilder) or [Object Mothers](https://wiki.c2.com/?ObjectMother).
